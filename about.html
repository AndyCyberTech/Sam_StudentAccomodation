About Sam Properties

Built by a landlord who lives in the same city as his tenants.

Sam Properties started with one simple idea: student residences in Westdene should be secure, well maintained and easy to deal with — no call centres, no vanishing landlords.

The short version

Three residences. One standard.

Sam Properties manages three residences in Westdene, Johannesburg, all within easy walking distance of UJ's Auckland Park Kingsway campus. Whether you're on NSFAS, a bursary, paying your own way, or working your first job, the same standard applies everywhere: secure access, working WiFi, and a pool to actually relax at.

Every resident deals directly with Sam or the on-site team — not a call centre — so maintenance issues and lease questions get sorted quickly.
